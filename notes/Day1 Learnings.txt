what is agent studio: 
Gainsight Agent Studio is a no-code workspace designed for teams to create and customize AI assistants 
using natural language. The platform automates routine tasks such as drafting emails and summarizing meetings 
while maintaining human oversight for review and approval

what is MCP for CS: 
In Customer Success (CS), MCP stands for Model Context Protocol. It is an open-source technical 
standard (originally introduced by Anthropic) that acts like a "universal USB port" for AI.It allows third-party AI tools 
like ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini to securely plug directly into your CS software (like Gainsight or Staircase AI) and read 
live customer data in real time, without needing custom engineering work.Why CS Teams Use MCPTraditionally, 
customer data (health scores, timeline updates, emails, and support tickets) stays trapped inside 
Customer Success Platforms (CSPs). If a Customer Success Manager wants to use ChatGPT to help write an email, 
they have to copy and paste customer details back and forth.MCP removes this barrier. 
It securely exposes Gainsight/CS data as "tools" that public AI models can look up dynamically.



what is Staircase AI:
Staircase AI (now part of Gainsight) is an AI platform that automatically analyzes communication data 
like emails and meetings. It tracks relationship health and sentiment shifts to predict customer churn risks and 
uncover hidden sales expansion opportunities.

what is Horizon AI: 
IT is the name used by two completely different software tools: an enterprise process finder and
a customer management engine.Depending on the version you are asking about, 
it means one of the following:
The Process Consultant (UseHorizon): An AI tool that interviews thousands of corporate employees at scale to automatically
find and fix internal company inefficiencies.
The Customer Engine (Gainsight Horizon AI): A built-in AI assistant for customer success teams that writes follow-up emails,
summarizes meetings, and flags client renewal risks

what is Renewal AI Agent:
A Renewal AI Agent is an autonomous software assistant designed to manage contract renewals and retention for a 
company's customers.While it can assist customer success teams by surfacing risks and writing renewal briefs,
 it is primarily used to completely take over the end-to-end renewal process for "long-tail" accounts—small, 
 unmanaged, or semi-managed customer accounts that do not have a dedicated, human Customer Success Manager (CSM)


what is NRR vs GRR?
Net Retention Rate (NRR) and Gross Retention Rate (GRR) are the two most critical revenue metrics used by subscription-based 
(SaaS) and Customer Success teams to measure account health and growth.The core difference is that NRR includes the revenue 
gains from expansion (upsells and cross-sells), while GRR ignores expansion and focuses purely on how well you retain your 
starting revenue.
 Example:
Imagine you start the year with ₹1,00,000 in revenue from a group of customers:Scenario: You lose ₹5,000 to churn,
 ₹5,000 to downgrades, but successfully upsell other clients in that group for an extra ₹20,000.

 NRR is : 100000+20000-5000+5000/100000 = 110%
 GRR is : 100000-5000-5000/100000 = 90%
 
 NRR measures your total existing revenue changes by including expansion, allowing the score to exceed 100% 
 and show organic growth. GRR isolates revenue retention by ignoring expansion entirely, 
 capping at 100% to strictly expose customer churn and contract downgrades.
